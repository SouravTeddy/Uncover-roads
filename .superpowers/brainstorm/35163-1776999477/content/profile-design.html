Design decisions summarised
  • FREE badge — grey outline, no fill. Neutral, not shameful.
  • PRO badge — gold gradient fill, same position. Signals status instantly.
  • Avatar ring — thin gold gradient ring for Pro users only.
  • Trip counter — visible only for free users, 3 dots showing usage.
  • Subscription row — orange "Upgrade" CTA for free; gold "Active ✓" for Pro.
  • OB card border — orange for free, gold for Pro (subtle but consistent with tier colour).
  • Community tab — muted/greyed in bottom nav, no interaction.
