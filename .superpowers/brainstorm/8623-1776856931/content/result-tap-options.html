Item #3 — Opening the Place Card from Search Results

When the user taps a museum in search results, how do they open the full details card?

A

Two-zone tap row

Left zone (icon + name) → zooms map to pin. Right zone (chevron / info icon →) → opens the full PinCard with rating, hours, etc.

🏛️ Musée d'Orsay 0.4 km · Rue de Lille ⓘ

Tap name → pin on map  |  Tap ⓘ → open full card

B

Tap → pin on map, then second tap on pin → card

First tap closes the list and moves to the pin on the map. The pin shows a small tooltip. A second tap on the pin (or the tooltip) opens the PinCard sheet.

🏛️ Musée d'Orsay
📍 Tap again on pin to see details →

Less info density, but familiar map interaction

C

Expanded inline row on tap

Tapping a search result expands the row inline to show rating, distance, and an "Open" button — without leaving the list. Tap "Open" to get the full card.

🏛️ Musée d'Orsay ★ 4.7
📍 0.4 km · Rue de Lille View Details →

Most info before committing, but more taps
