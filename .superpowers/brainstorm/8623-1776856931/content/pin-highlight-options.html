Pin Highlight on Zoom-in (#2)

User taps ☕ cafe while zoomed out → zooms in → cafe pins appear. How do they highlight?

A

Ripple ring — one pulse outward

Each matching pin plays a single expanding ring animation (like a sonar ping) when results load after zoom. Ring fades out over ~600ms. Pin stays normal after.

Subtle, plays once per pin, staggered by ~100ms each

B

Glow burst — pin briefly enlarges + glows

Each pin scales up to 1.4× and gets a colored glow, then returns to normal over ~400ms. More attention-grabbing than a ring.

Plays once, heavier visual impact

C

Drop-in — pins animate in from above

Matching pins "drop" onto the map with a small bounce when they load after zoom, like being placed on the map. No ring or glow — movement is the signal.

Clean, no extra visual noise after landing

Zoom-in nudge (shown when bbox too large):
🔍 Showing cafes in this area. Zoom in for more accurate results.
