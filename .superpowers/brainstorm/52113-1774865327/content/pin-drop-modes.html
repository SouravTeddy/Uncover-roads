P1

Sheet collapses to handle bar

Full map visible, orange pin tracks map center, "Confirm" button at bottom. User pans the map to position the pin, then confirms. Most control, most screen space.

P2

Sheet stays, tap in the visible map strip

Sheet stays up showing context. A small map strip above the sheet is tappable. Faster (one tap), but the tappable area is small and you can't pan.
