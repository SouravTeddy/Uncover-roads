C — Category icon pins + glow route

Pins show category emoji (🏛️ museum, 🍜 food, etc). Route has a soft glow layer. Detour in red with a "DETOUR" label badge. Most expressive.

✈️ Multi-city hop animation (all options)
Option i — Plane arc
Map zooms out → dashed arc line draws from City A to City B → small ✈️ icon flies along the arc → map zooms into City B.
Option ii — Morph zoom
Map smoothly zooms out to show both cities, a connection line pulses between them, then re-zooms into next city. No plane icon — pure map motion.
Option iii — Overlay card
A "Flying to Mumbai →" card slides up over the map briefly, map cross-fades to new city. Simpler but less dramatic.

💡 My recommendation: Pin style A (numbered + color-coded — unambiguous, scannable at small map sizes) + animation i (plane arc — tells the story clearly, earns the delight moment).
