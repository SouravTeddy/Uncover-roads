Design Notes
1

Search — smart, unified

Single search handles cities and places. "Paris" loads the map for Paris. "Eiffel Tower" jumps to that pin. "cafés near me" uses the location button. Rotating placeholders (already built in MapScreen) carry over here.

2

In Progress — 3-layer mix

City hero card (photo + city name + resume button) → chips for quick place access → compact draft banner showing progress dots → horizontal place card scroll. Only visible when there are selected places. Hidden with a clean CTA when empty.

3

Discover carousel — A + D

Alternates between editorial cards ("Best time to visit Kyoto") and stat cards ("€52/day in Lisbon", "#1 Bangkok"). No AI/persona inference. Curated, real data. Different card accent colours per type.

4

Session restore fix

getInitialScreen() always returns 'destination' (this screen) instead of restoring to 'map'. The last map state (city, places, filter) is still preserved in localStorage — it just surfaces here first, not directly on the map.

5

Empty state

When there's no in-progress activity, the "In Progress" section is replaced by a gentle nudge — "Where are you heading?" with a large illustrated prompt. The Discover carousel is always visible.

6

Animations planned

Entrance shimmer on place cards, carousel auto-scroll with pause-on-hover, subtle gradient pulse on city hero card, smooth slide-in for the In Progress section when activity exists.
