What changed and why
KEEP
Hero image, title, rating, open/closed — core decision signals. Rating earns trust. Open/closed is time-critical. Moved closing time into the hero badge so it's impossible to miss.
CUT
Google editorial summary — generic copy ("built in 1656 by Shah Jahan") that says nothing about why *this user* should go. Replaced by "Why this for you."
CUT
Type chips + Wikipedia link — zero decision value at this stage. User is choosing whether to add, not researching. Wikipedia sends them out of the app.
CUT
Google Maps link — we are the map. Linking out to Google from a map app is a trust leak. Directions (deep link) is kept because it's genuinely useful.
NEW
"Why this for you" — persona-matched reason. Cultural fit, sequence logic, budget signal. This is the moat. One sentence that Google can never show.
NEW
Max 2 intel pills — only the most critical live signal. Time alert (closing early) + crowd level. Never more than 2 — any more becomes noise at this card size.
NEW
Local tip — one curated insider line from the LLM. Specific, actionable, not in any guide. This is what a friend who's been there says.
NEW
Area label under name — "Old Delhi" instead of just category. Adds location context without a separate address line.
