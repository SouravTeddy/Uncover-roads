What changed in v2
1

Search — isolated by border

A subtle border-bottom line separates the search bar from everything below. Clean and familiar — like every good app's top bar.

2

In Progress — card with purple tint

Rounded card container with a warm purple background tint and border. Section title "IN PROGRESS" in purple. Everything inside (city hero, chips, draft banner, photo scroll) lives inside this one card — so it reads as a single block.

Inner dividers (hairline lines) separate the sub-rows within it without making them feel like separate sections.

3

Discover — card with blue tint

Separate rounded card with a cool blue background tint. Section title "DISCOVER" in blue. The color difference alone makes the boundary obvious even without any explicit divider line.

4

Why cards, not lines?

Divider lines between sections feel dated and flat. Tinted cards give each section its own spatial identity — purple = "your stuff", blue = "explore new". Users immediately associate color with context, no label needed.
