Question card design direction

Three approaches to displaying onboarding choices — all support multi-select. Which feels right for Uncover Roads?

Q · How do you ease into the day?
Coffee shop ritual
Sit-down breakfast
Straight to it

A — Text rows

Full-width stacked rows. One tap = selected. Checkbox at right. Clean, screenreader-friendly. Easy to scan vertically. Works well for 4–6 options.

Q · What's the trip mood?
Explore & discover Eat & drink ✓ Relax & recharge Deep culture ✓ Nightlife

B — Pill chips

Compact, wrapping pills. Feels like tagging. Great for 5–8 options. Very fast to scan & tap. Less accessible (small tap targets). Best for secondary / adjective-style answers.

Q · How do you pace a day?
🌿 Slow & deep 2–3 stops/day
⚡ Pack it in 6–8 stops/day
⚖️ Balanced 4–5 stops/day
🎲 Spontaneous 3 anchors + gaps

C — Icon + label grid

2×2 or 2×3 grid. Emoji/icon as anchor. Label + descriptor. Good for 4–6 options. More visual than text rows but lighter than full image cards. Works with multi-select.
